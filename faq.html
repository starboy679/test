Frequently Asked Questions

How does the ordering process work?

Choose a ready-made gift or design your own via our website. Then, chat with us on WhatsApp to finalize details, payment, and delivery.

What is the turnaround time?

Ready-made gifts are prepared within 24-48 hours. Custom gifts take 3-5 working days to design and prepare.

How do I pay?

Payment is handled securely via WhatsApp. We accept cash on delivery, bank transfer, or mobile wallet options.

Do you deliver across Egypt?

Yes, we deliver to all locations in Egypt. Delivery fees may apply depending on the location.

Can I make changes or cancel my order?

Changes can be made up to 24 hours before preparation starts. Cancellations are accepted with full refund if not yet prepared.

How do you handle privacy of messages and photos?

All personal messages and uploaded images are treated with the utmost confidentiality and used only for your gift preparation.

What if I have a special request?

Contact us on WhatsApp with your ideas. We're happy to accommodate special requests where possible.
